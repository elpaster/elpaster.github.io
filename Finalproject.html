Designing for All: The Power of Universal Design

Creating an internet that works for everyone — future designers start here.

Introduction diversity_3

The web keeps evolving, but big changes have always come from us — designers, coders, and creators who want better for the internet. From CSS Zen Garden, to Web Standards, to HTML5 semantics, progress always begins with a movement.

The next movement? Universal Design and Accessibility. If the internet is the world's library, classroom, workplace, and playground… then everyone deserves access to it.

This site began as a learning project aimed at demonstrating accessible web practices while teaching the core principles of inclusive design. Dive into its three pages to discover the what, how, and why of Universal Design.
